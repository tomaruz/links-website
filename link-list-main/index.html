What is on this Website?

As a designer, I like to use websites such as Dribbble and Behance, for when I need to feel inspired for my own creative projects. I enjoy looking at and dissecting other designers' work as it helps me understand the processes and reasoning behind their creative work.

This website also contains a list of links to creative agencies' websites that I discovered, fell in love with and would like to work for in the future! I love going through their websites as they tend to document their processes well and explain their creative thinking to solve each brief.

I have also decided to create a gallery of design work that I found clever and inspiring. The hopefully ever-growing collection of work is a selection of my favourite design projects that I discovered and loved!
